关于我 About Me

我是个挺资深的游戏玩家，尤其喜欢 2D 像素风的作品。那种由像素拼起来的小世界，总有种很独特的吸引力。《Minecraft》也一直在我的常玩列表里——每次打开都像进入一个可以随意延展的空间，慢慢搭、慢慢探索，很容易一玩就是很久。

I’ve been into games for quite a while, especially 2D pixel-style ones. There’s a certain charm in those tiny, handcrafted worlds that never really fades. Minecraft is also something I keep coming back to — every time I load it up, it feels like stepping into a space where I can just build, explore, and lose track of time.

音乐基本是每天都离不开的东西。平时听得最多的是中文歌，歌词里的情绪很细腻，也更容易让我产生共鸣。一个人待着的时候会更偏向纯音乐，没有具体的表达，反而更容易让人放空。

Music is something I listen to pretty much every day. I mostly go for Chinese songs — the emotions in the lyrics just hit closer to home. When I’m alone, I tend to switch to instrumentals. Without words, it’s easier to just let my mind wander a bit.

我也会看一些动漫。遇到特别喜欢的作品，会反复看很多遍。倒不是因为记不住剧情，而是每次重看都会注意到之前没发现的细节，感觉也会有点不一样。

I also watch anime from time to time. When I really like a series, I’ll rewatch it more than once. Not because I forget the story, but because each time I notice something new, or just feel it a little differently.
