关于我： About Me:

我是一个热爱探索的玩家，对 2D 像素风游戏情有独钟。那些由方块和像素点构筑的微观世界，总有一种让人沉浸其中的独特魔力。同时，《Minecraft》也是我无法割舍的自留地——在那个无限延伸的沙盒里，创造与探索永无止境。

I am an avid gamer with a deep affection for 2D pixel-art games. There is a unique, captivating charm in those micro-worlds built from simple pixels. Minecraft is also my ultimate sanctuary — an infinitely expanding sandbox where exploration and creativity truly know no bounds.

音乐是我生活中的情绪解药。我喜欢听中文歌，偏爱那份藏在字里行间的细腻情感与共鸣；而在独处或是需要放空的时候，纯音乐便成了最好的白噪音。没有了歌词的束缚，思绪反而能飞得更远。

Music is the emotional antidote in my daily life. I love listening to Chinese songs, drawn to the delicate emotions and resonance woven into the lyrics. When I'm alone or need to unwind, instrumental music becomes my perfect ambient companion — without the anchor of words, my thoughts are free to wander.

闲暇时，我也喜欢沉浸在动漫的世界里。我不盲从热门，只看随缘或是真正感兴趣的故事。遇到触动内心的神作，我会忍不住N刷——每一次重温，不仅能剥离出新的剧情细节，更能照见不同心境下的自己。

In my free time, I love immersing myself in the world of anime. I don't follow trends blindly; I only watch what genuinely catches my eye. When I encounter a masterpiece that touches my heart, I will rewatch it again and again — not just to spot new details, but to experience the story through the lens of a different state of mind.
