关于我：About Me:

我是个骨灰级游戏爱好者，尤其钟情于 2D 像素风格的游戏——那些像素点拼成的小世界，总有一种说不清的魔力让我流连忘返。《Minecraft》也是我的心头好，每次打开都像进入一个可以无限延伸的创作空间，永远有新东西可以探索和建造。

I'm a dedicated gamer at heart, with a particular love for 2D pixel-art games — there's something inexplicably magical about those tiny pixel worlds that keeps pulling me back. Minecraft is another favorite of mine; every session feels like stepping into an infinitely expandable creative space, always with something new to explore or build.

听歌对我来说是日常必需品。中文歌是我的主食，歌词里那种细腻的情感表达是其他语言很难替代的；纯音乐则是我独处时的最佳伴侣，没有歌词，反而能让思绪更自由地漂浮。

Music is a daily necessity for me. Chinese songs are my staple — the emotional nuance in their lyrics is something other languages rarely match. Instrumental music, on the other hand, is my best companion in solitude; without lyrics, it lets my thoughts drift more freely.

动漫也是我生活的一部分。我喜欢追自己感兴趣的作品，遇到真正打动我的，会反复重看好几遍——不是因为忘了剧情，而是每一次都能发现新的细节，感受到不同的东西。

Anime is woven into my everyday life as well. When I find a series that genuinely moves me, I'll watch it through multiple times — not because I forgot the plot, but because each rewatch reveals new details and brings something different each time.
